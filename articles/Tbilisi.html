Capital of Georgia - Tbilisi


Discover the captivating blend of history, culture, and modernity in Tbilisi, the vibrant capital city of Georgia. Situated at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, Tbilisi beckons travelers with its rich tapestry of experiences.


Begin your journey in the heart of the city, where the historic Old Town invites you to wander its narrow cobblestone streets lined with colorful balconies and ancient churches. Marvel at the iconic landmarks such as the Narikala Fortress, which offers panoramic views of the cityscape and the meandering Mtkvari River below.


As you explore further, immerse yourself in the diverse cultural heritage of Tbilisi. Admire the intricate architecture of the Holy Trinity Cathedral, a symbol of Georgia's spiritual identity, and visit the Sioni Cathedral, adorned with stunning frescoes and ornate carvings.


Indulge your senses in Tbilisi's bustling markets, where the aroma of freshly baked bread and spices fills the air. Sample traditional Georgian cuisine, including khachapuri (cheese-filled bread) and khinkali (dumplings), at cozy local eateries scattered throughout the city.


Experience the city's modern side in the trendy neighborhoods of Rustaveli Avenue and Vera, where chic cafes, art galleries, and boutiques showcase Tbilisi's contemporary flair. Don't miss the chance to unwind in the city's famous sulfur baths, a centuries-old tradition beloved by locals and visitors alike.


Throughout your stay, Tbilisi will enchant you with its warm hospitality and boundless charm, leaving you with memories to last a lifetime. Whether you're strolling through its historic streets, savoring its culinary delights, or embracing its vibrant culture, Tbilisi promises an unforgettable journey for every traveler.
