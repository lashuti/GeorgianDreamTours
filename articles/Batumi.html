Batumi: Jewel of the Black Sea


Batumi, nestled on the picturesque coast of the Black Sea, is a jewel of Georgia's seaside resorts, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, vibrant culture, and historical charm.


Explore the lush greenery and cascading waterfalls of the surrounding region, starting with a visit to the quaint village of Mirveti. Here, the stunning Mirveti Waterfall captivates visitors with its serene beauty, creating a tranquil oasis amidst the verdant landscapes.


Just a short distance away lies the majestic Makhuntseti Waterfall, where the rushing waters plunge into a natural pool below, inviting travelers to cool off and rejuvenate in its refreshing embrace.


For nature enthusiasts, Batumi offers a haven of botanical wonders at the Batumi Botanical Garden. Spanning over 108 hectares, this verdant paradise boasts an impressive collection of flora from around the world, including exotic plants, rare species, and vibrant flowers.


Continue your botanical journey at the Dendrological Park, a verdant oasis nestled in the heart of Batumi. Stroll along winding pathways shaded by towering trees, and marvel at the diverse collection of plants, shrubs, and flowers that thrive in this tranquil setting.


For those seeking adventure, venture into the lush landscapes of Mtirala National Park, where dense forests, cascading streams, and diverse wildlife await. Hike through pristine wilderness, discover hidden waterfalls, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this protected area.


Whether you're exploring the enchanting waterfalls, wandering through botanical gardens, or embarking on outdoor adventures in Mtirala National Park, Batumi promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler.
