Georgia is distinguished by the many different kinds of extreme sports. You can engage in mountain, marine, river, and air adventure sports. The diversity of Georgian nature allows a person to experience the adrenaline-rush activity at least once in a lifetime.


Here are adventure sports below you can engage in Georgia:

Horse Riding Tours

The mountainous regions of Georgia are distinguished by horse riding tours. Where cars cannot reach, horses can take you. You will meet many such tours in almost all mountainous parts of Georgia - in national parks, valleys, or places inaccessible to cars.

Skiing

There are many alpine-skiing sports in Georgia. If you visit the well-known mountain resorts of Gudauri and Bakuriani in winter, you will have a chance to ski. At the resorts, you will find the ski runs and infrastructure appropriate for skiing.

It is also not long ago that amateurs became interested in snowboarding. It is easier than skiing, but it takes a few days to master. It is also interesting to note that there are different types of snowboarding, which makes this sport more attractive.

Zipline

Zipline is becoming more and more popular in Georgia. It is a stretched steel cable between two points on which the so-called roller slides down and you move from one point to another. You will meet the zipline both in Tbilisi and in the regions. Its price depends on the location and the length of the zipline.

Velo Tour

Cycling is one of the components of a healthy lifestyle. The number of people who engage in cycling on an extreme trail is growing in Georgia. Both organized and amateur Velo tours are organized throughout Georgia. This sport allows you to choose a difficult or easy trail and start an unforgettable adventure with your friends.

Parachuting

This kind of sport does not have a long history in Georgia. It involves jumping out of a plane and landing peacefully on the ground after launching a parachute. Parachuting is quite an expensive pleasure, however, if the adrenaline-rush experience is a priority for you, parachuting will induce plentiful adrenaline.

Paragliding

Paragliding will also give you the amazing feeling of flying in the air. This sport exists in Georgia for a long time. There is also a paragliding federation in Georgia, which offers special flights. Paragliding is possible at several locations, including the Tbilisi Sea, Gudauri , Sighnaghi, and others.

Air Balloon

If you are not afraid of heights, then enjoying panoramic views from 150 meters in a hot air balloon will be an unforgettable experience. It is possible to fly with a hot-air balloon only in Tbilisi, but the number of visitors is always high. The flight lasts only 15 minutes but evokes unforgettable emotions in everyone.

Rafting

Summer is that time of the year when we all try to discover something new, create unforgettable memories, and refresh ourselves. Surprisingly, all this can be combined in one activity - Rafting.
Rafting in Georgia has been especially popular in the last decade. Due to the landscape of Georgia, rafting is possible in many regions.
If you are an extreme lover, then you should definitely try rafting down the different rivers of Georgia.
Rafting is becoming more and more popular among the extreme water sports in Georgia. The improvement of the sport is greatly influenced by the abundance of rivers in Georgia. Rafting is possible on Aragvi, Mtkvari, Rioni, and Adjaristskali. Rafting has categories according to the difficulty by which the type of swimming is determined. It is a summer activity and is carried out with the help of a professional instructor.
