Itenarary

Day 1 - Arrival to Tbilisi

  • Warm welcome at the airport, transfer to the hotel
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 2 - It is time to explore Tbilisi city tour, visiting main attractions of the city:

  • 10:00 Start of the Tour in Tbilisi (time can be discussed with the guide)
  • Starting the tour from the Holy Trinity Cathedral commonly known as Sameba Cathedral
Then we move forward to the old city. Here we will visit the below landmarks:
  • Abanotubani - the ancient district of Tbilisi, known for its sulfuric baths.
  • Leghvtakhevi waterfall which is 22 meters tall.
  • Narikala Fortress Metekhi
  • Rike Park
  • Once we take lots of pictures and enjoy the area of old town, we drive to Mtatsminda Park.
  • Tour will be finished around 16:00
  • Time at leisure
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 3 - Departure to Batumi thorugh Prometheus Cave

  • Departure to Batumi
  • Stop at Prometheus Cave - The total length of the cave is about 11 km, of which 1060 m are open to visitors. The cave has a total of 22 halls, six of which are currently open to see
  • Second stop near the monument of friendship built in Gudauriopening where opens beautiful views from the mountains
  • Drop off to Batumi hotel
  • Overnight in Batumi

Day 4 - Batumi

Batumi city tour, visiting some of the main sightseeing places:
  • Europe Square is surrounded by beautiful belle époque buildings
  • The Medea monument
  • The Astronomical Clock
  • Ali & Nino The 7m-high, ethereally moving, metal sculpture of a Woman and a Man, by Tamar Kvesitadze
  • Alphabet Tower - a 130-meter-high structure which symbolizes the uniqueness of the Georgian alphabet and people
  • Finish by 14:00
  • Time at leisure
  • Overnight in Batumi

Day 5 - Adjara - Makhuntseti Waterfall

  • Breakfast in Hotel
  • 10:00 Start of the Tour
  • Makhuntseti Waterfall is one of the most popular waterfalls in Georgia. It is located in Adjara, in the Makhuntseti village. This is one of the highest waterfalls in Adjara - a real pride of local residents
  • Tour will be finished around 15:00
  • Time at leisure
  • Overnight in Batumi

Day 6 - Departure to Tbilisi through Mtskheta and Jvari monastery

  • Departure to Tbilisi
  • First Stop at Mtskheta
  • Second Stop at Jvari monastery
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 7 - Trip on Georgian Military road to Kazbegi

  • 10:00 Starting the Tour
  • Trip on Georgian Military road to Kazbegi
  • First stop at Zhinvali reservoir
  • Second stop near the monument of friendship built in Gudauri - Mesmerizing views from the mountains
  • Visiting Gergety Trinity Church in Kazbegi
  • Tour will be finished around 19:00
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 8 - Transfer to Tbilisi airport

  • Check out from hotel
  • Transfer to the airport
