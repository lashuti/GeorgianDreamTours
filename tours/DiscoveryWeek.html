Georgian Gems: A Week of Discovery
6 nights & 7 days


Embark on a week-long journey through Georgia's most captivating destinations with our "Georgian Gems" tour. Over 6 nights and 7 days, immerse yourself in the vibrant streets of Tbilisi before exploring the ancient wonders of Ananuri. Traverse the majestic mountains of Gudauri and Bakuriani, where breathtaking vistas await at every turn. Indulge in the healing waters of Borjomi and uncover the hidden treasures of the region's enchanting canyons. Conclude your adventure back in Tbilisi, having experienced a week of discovery through Georgia's diverse landscapes and cultural wonders with a Local Guide


Included

  • Airport Chauffeurs
  • Accomodation in the Hotel (including Breakfast)
  • Transportation during the tour in a comfortable car (Different types of vehicles are available depending on the number of Guests)
  • Professional Local Guide

Itenarary

Day 1 - Arrival to Tbilisi

  • Warm welcome at the airport, transfer to the hotel
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 2 - It is time to explore Tbilisi city tour, visiting main attractions of the city:

  • 10:00 Start of the Tour in Tbilisi (time can be discussed with the guide)
  • Starting the tour from the Holy Trinity Cathedral commonly known as Sameba Cathedral
Then we move forward to the old city. Here we will visit the below landmarks:
  • Abanotubani - the ancient district of Tbilisi, known for its sulfuric baths.
  • Leghvtakhevi waterfall which is 22 meters tall. Narikala Fortress Metekhi - Rike Park
  • Once we take lots of pictures and enjoy the area of old town, we drive to Mtatsminda Park.
  • Tour will be finished around 16:00
  • Time at leisure
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 3 - Trip on Georgian Military road to Kazbegi

  • 10:00 Start of the Tour
  • Trip on Georgian Military road to Kazbegi
  • First stop at Zhinvali reservoir
  • Second stop near the monument of friendship built in Gudauriopening where opens beautiful views from the mountains
  • Visiting Gergety Trinity Church in Kazbegi
  • Tour will be finished around 19:00
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 4 - Bakuriani & Borjomi

  • Departure to Borjomi via Bakuriani
  • Bakuriani - The mountain resort Bakuriani is situated at an elevation of 1,700 meters (5576 feet) above sea level in the sub-tropical zone.
  • Continue journey to Borjomi
  • Borjomi is a resort town noted for mineral water industry, the Romanov summer palace in Likani, and the World Wide Fund for Nature-site Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park
  • Overnight in Borjomi

Day 5 - Martvili & Okatse Canyons

  • Trip to Samegrelo and visiting Martvili canyon - the miracle of the nature, where you have the chance to ride the boats there with amazing waterfalls
  • Okatse Canyon is natural monument with recently built high quality tourist infrastructure - stairs, footpaths offering magnificent views to the canyon and its surroundings make a nice day out in the nature especially in spring when water levels are high and nature is in blossom
  • Overnight in Martvili

Day 6 - UNESCO Heritage places Jvari Monastery & Svetitskhoveli Church

  • Visiting UNESCO Heritage places Jvari Monastery Svetitskhoveli Church It is currently the second largest church building in Georgia
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 7 - Transfer to Tbilisi airport

  • Check out from hotel at 12 noon
  • Free time before departure
  • Transfer to the airport.
