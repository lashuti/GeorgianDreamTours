Itenarary

Day 1 - Arrival to Tbilisi

  • Warm welcome at the airport, transfer to the hotel
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 2 - Capitals of Georgia - Tbilisi and Mtskheta Tour

  • 10:00 Start of the Tour in Tbilisi (time can be discussed with the guide)
  • Starting the tour from the Holy Trinity Cathedral commonly known as Sameba Cathedral
Then we move forward to the old city. Here we will visit the below landmarks:
  • Metekhi plateau with the view of Old Town
  • Rike Park and Bridge of Peace (The glass bridge)
  • Cable car to Narikala Fortress, the main landmark of Tbilisi - An ancient fort defending city and opening breathtaking views of the capital
  • Abanotubani (baths district) - the oldest district of the capital where it's history starts from
  • Leghvtakhevi waterfall - a beautiful nature wonder in the heart of old city
  • Lunch time
  • Trip to Mtskheta - the first capital of Georgia
    • Visiting UNESCO Heritage places
    • Jvari Monastery
  • Svetitskhoveli Church is an Eastern Orthodox cathedral
  • Tour will be finished around 18:00
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 3 - Borjomi Resort

  • Departure to Borjomi
  • Borjomi is a resort town noted for mineral water industry, the Romanov summer palace in Likani, and the World Wide Fund for Nature-site Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park
  • Overnight in Borjomi

Day 4 - Departure to Batumi via canyons

  • Departure to Batumi via canyon
  • Trip to Samegrelo and visiting Martvili canyon - the miracle of the nature, where you have the chance to ride the boats there with amazing waterfalls all around you
  • Overnight in Batumi

Day 6 - Departure to Tbilisi via Jvari Monastery

  • Departure to Tbilisi
  • Jvari - the masterpiece of architecture Jvari is dated 585-604 cc AD. Located on the hill top near the town Mtskheta, it is listed in UNESCO World Heritage Site list in 1994. From Jvari you can enjoy an amazing view of confluence (merging) of 2 different rivers: Mtkvari and Aragvi
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 7 - Farewell to Georgia

  • Check out from hotel
  • Transfer to the airport
  • Farewell to Georgia
