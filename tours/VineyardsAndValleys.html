Itenarary

Day 1 - Arrival to Tbilisi

  • Warm welcome at the airport, transfer to the hotel
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 2 - It is time to explore Tbilisi city tour, visiting main attractions of the city:

  • 10:00 Start of the Tour in Tbilisi (time can be discussed with the guide)
  • Starting the tour from the Holy Trinity Cathedral commonly known as Sameba Cathedral
Then we move forward to the old city. Here we will visit the below landmarks:
  • Metekhi plateau with the view of Old Town
  • Rike Park and Bridge of Peace (The glass bridge)
  • Cable car to Narikala Fortress, the main landmark of Tbilisi - An ancient fort defending city and opening breathtaking views of the capital
  • Abanotubani (baths district) - the oldest district of the capital where it's history starts from
  • Leghvtakhevi waterfall - a beautiful nature wonder in the heart of old city
  • Lunch time
  • Trip to Mtskheta - the first capital of Georgia
  • Visiting UNESCO Heritage places Jvari Monastery
  • Svetitskhoveli Church is an Eastern Orthodox cathedral
  • Tour will be finished around 18:00
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 3 - Kakheti Region

  • 10:00 Start of the Tour
  • Day trip to eastern part of Georgia - Kakheti region, where you can enjoy the mountain scenery, picturesque villages, ancient temples, taste unique Georgian wines and get acquainted with the secrets of wine-making
    • KTW wine cellar. Wine excursion and tasting.
    • BADIAURI - Georgian bread baking masterclasses and mountain cheese tasting.
  • The Monastery of St. Nino at Bodbe is a Georgian Orthodox monastic complex originally built in the 9th century, it has been significantly remodeled, especially in the 17th century. The monastery now functions as a nunnery and is one of the major pilgrimage sites in Georgia, due to its association with St. Nino, the 4th-century female evangelist of Georgians, whose relics are shrined there
  • Sighnaghi is endowed with significant historic and cultural heritage sites. Its charming cobblestone streets and alleys, restored original nouveau woodwork, and detailed facades give the town a romantic mystique.The town sits atop a bluff overlooking the Alazani Valley, which is one of Georgia's most famous wine-producing regions. Stunning view of the Alazani Valley make Sighnaghi one of Georgia's most attractive small towns.
  • Lunch/dinner during this tour can be planned upon wish and preference of the guests
  • Back to Tbilisi from the trip around evening time
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 4 - Transfer to Tbilisi airport

  • Breakfast in the Hotel
  • Check out from hotel
  • Transfer to Tbilisi airport
  • Farewell to Georgia
