The Perfect Vacation
8 nights & 9 days


Experience the ultimate vacation in Georgia with our "Perfect Vacation" tour, spanning 8 nights and 9 days of unforgettable adventures. Begin your journey in the vibrant capital of Tbilisi before delving into the ancient history of Gori and exploring the ancient cave city of Uplistsikhe. Discover the cultural treasures of Kutaisi, including the majestic Gelati Monastery and the breathtaking Prometheus Cave, before marveling at the natural wonders of Martvili Canyon. Journey to the remote mountain villages of Mestia and Ushguli, UNESCO World Heritage Sites renowned for their ancient stone towers and stunning alpine scenery. Explore the historic city of Zugdidi before heading to the charming coastal city of Batumi, where you can relax on pristine beaches, explore botanical gardens, and experience vibrant nightlife. Conclude your perfect vacation with a return to Tbilisi, having experienced the best of Georgia's history, culture, and natural beauty.

Itinerary

Day 1 - Tbilisi City Tour

  • Time to explore one of the oldest, aesthetic, and authentic cities in Europe!
  • Start with the holy trinity cathedral, one of the most important landmarks of Tbilisi.
  • Combine walking and riding the aerial cable car from Rike Park to the fortress of Narikala.
  • Explore the sulfur bathhouse district - Abanotubani, followed by Maidan Square, Shardeni, and King Erekle II streets.
  • Pass by Sioni Cathedral and cross the famous contemporary symbol of Tbilisi - The Bridge of Peace.
  • Visit the Orbeliani Square and Atoneli Palace, the official residence of the president of Georgia.
  • Explore the historical spots of Tbilisi - Dry Bridge, also known as The Place Where Memories are Sold.

Day 2 - Tbilisi -> Gori -> Uplistsikhe -> Kutaisi

  • Drive to the Western side of Georgia through Gori, the heart of the Kartli region.
  • Visit the museum of Joseph Stalin in Gori, consisting of Stalin’s memorial house and personal belongings.
  • Explore the ancient cave complex of Uplistsikhe, one of the oldest urban settlements in Georgia.
  • Continue to the Imereti region and arrive in Kutaisi, the second-largest city in Georgia.
  • Walking tour in Kutaisi is optional.

Day 3 - Kutaisi -> Prometheus Cave -> Martvili Canyons -> Enguri Dam -> Mestia

  • Drive to Prometheus cave, a karst cave with mysterious vibes and stunning formations.
  • Explore Prometheus cave with its 22 halls, six of which are open to tourists.
  • Continue to Martvili Canyons, the sight of local waterfalls, and take a boat trip in the river Abasha.
  • Have a small stop at Enguri Dam, the largest dam in Georgia and one of the biggest Soviet projects in the region.
  • Ride through Svanetian mountains and arrive in Mestia, the heart of the Svaneti region.

Day 4 - Mestia -> Ushguli -> Mestia

  • Start an off-road adventure to Ushguli, one of the highest settlements in Europe.
  • Stop at Love Tower and explore Ushguli, visiting Lamaria Church and King Tamar's winter residence.
  • Enjoy the scenery and view of the highest mountains of Caucasus.
  • Walk through narrow paths between ancient towers and enjoy free time in Ushguli.

Day 5 - Mestia -> Zugdidi -> Batumi

  • Head to Svaneti Museum of History and Ethnography in Mestia.
  • Drive to Zugdidi and visit Dadiani Palace, a museum established by Prince David Dadiani.
  • Explore Dadiani Palace and learn about Georgian royal family.
  • Continue to Batumi, the capital city and port of the Adjara region.

Day 6 - Batumi City Tour

  • Visit Batumi Botanical Garden with panoramic views over the Black Sea shores and numerous indigenous plants.

Day 7 - Batumi -> Kutaisi

  • Head to Kutaisi and have a free evening there.

Day 8 - Kutaisi -> Mtskheta -> Tbilisi

  • Visit Mtskheta town and explore its UNESCO World Heritage Sites – Svetitskhoveli Cathedral and Jvari Monastery.
  • Conclusional dinner in Mtskheta.
  • Return to Tbilisi.

Day 9 - Departure

  • Check out from the hotel and transfer back to the airport.
