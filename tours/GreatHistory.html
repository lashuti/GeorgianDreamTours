The Country of Great History
3 nights & 4 days


Embark on a captivating journey through Georgia's rich history with our "Georgia: The Country of Great History" tour. Over 3 nights and 4 days, delve into the ancient streets of Tbilisi before exploring the historic wonders of Mtskheta and Ananuri. Admire the majestic landscapes of Gudauri and Kazbegi, where towering mountains meet timeless traditions. Immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of Georgia as you visit historic sites and scenic vistas. Experience the allure of Georgia's great history and conclude your journey back in Tbilisi, filled with memories of this storied land.


Included

  • Airport Chauffeurs
  • Accomodation in the Hotel (including Breakfast)
  • Transportation during the tour in a comfortable car (Different types of vehicles are available depending on the number of Guests)
  • Professional Local Guide
  • SUVs (4x4 car) to Gergeti Trinity Church

Itenarary

Day - 1 Arrival to Tbilisi

  • Warm welcome at the airport, transfer to the hotel
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 2 - It is time to explore Tbilisi city tour, visiting main attractions of the city:

  • Metekhi plateau with the view of Old Town
  • Rike park and Bridge of Peace (The glass bridge)
  • Cable car to Narikala Fortress, Leghvtakhevi waterfall
Trip to Mtskheta - the first capital of Georgia
  • Visiting UNESCO Heritage places Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Church
  • Tour will be finished around 18:00
  • Overnight in Tbilisi

Day 3 - Trip on Georgian Military road to Kazbegi

  • During this trip you will enjoy beautiful mountain views and fresh air
  • First stop at Zhinvali reservoir near Ananuri fortress - architectural complex of the late feudal times in Georgia.
  • Second stop in Gudauri ski resort - 2196 meters above the sea. Where you have a possibility of paragliding
  • Visiting Gergety Trinity Church in Kazbegi
  • Tour will be finished around 19:00

Day 4 - Transfer to Tbilisi airport

  • Farewell to Georgia
