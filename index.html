Adjara - Batumi

Welcome to Batumi, the dynamic coastal gem of Adjara, where the Black Sea meets urban excitement amidst lush greenery, waterfalls, and national parks. Explore charming streets, bustling promenades, and iconic landmarks, immersing yourself in its vibrant atmosphere.

Kakheti

Welcome to Kakheti, Georgia's famed wine region, where vineyards, villages, and scenic landscapes beckon. Explore historic wineries, cultural landmarks, and lush vineyards, indulging in the rich wine-making heritage and natural splendor of this idyllic area.
